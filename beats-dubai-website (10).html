OUR STORY
One Person.
One City.
One Mission.

DJ RADAR (Renjith C.B) is a Dubai-based DJ with 17+ years inside the UAE's tourism, event, and entertainment industries.

In 2023 — before Untold Dubai, before Tomorrowland — he submitted a proposal directly to the CEO of Dubai Festivals and Retail Establishment. Dubai Tourism's senior leadership personally engaged. The Zoom call was confirmed.

BEATS DUBAI: Frequencies of Peace is the evolution of that vision. A music movement that answers conflict with culture, community, and sound.

THE JOURNEY
How We Got Here
AUG 2023
Dubai Electroverse Proposal Submitted
DJ RADAR submitted the world's first Dubai-focused mega EDM festival concept directly to Ahmed Al Khaja, CEO of Dubai Festivals and Retail Establishment. The CEO's office personally replied within days.
SEP 2023
Dubai Tourism Zoom Call Confirmed
Miguel Graciani, Senior Manager Festivals and Events Development, reviewed the 31-slide deck, provided the official sponsorship portal, and confirmed a 30-minute Zoom call for September 25, 2023.
2023–25
Dubai's Festival Economy Launches
Untold Dubai, Tomorrowland, Ushuaia, Terrasolisis all launch — validating exactly what DJ RADAR proposed. The vision seeded an entire ecosystem.
APR 2026
BEATS DUBAI: Frequencies of Peace Launches
The vision evolves. A music movement with a humanitarian mission, a talent platform, a loyalty system, and a community that moves together.
